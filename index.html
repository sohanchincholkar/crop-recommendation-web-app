The Future of Agriculture is here

Agriculture is first and foremost factor which is important for survival. Machine learning (ML) could be a crucial perspective for acquiring real-world and operative solution for crop prediction issue.

This project focuses mainly on predicting the crop by applying various machine learning techniques.The prediction made by machine learning algorithms will help the farmers to come to a decision which crop to grow to induce the most yield by considering factors like temperature, rainfall, humidity etc. This bridges the gap between technology and agriculture sector.

This is the future of Agriculture.
